Delicious Tres Leches Cake Recipe

Estimated Time Frame

Time to prepare the cake: 15 minutes

Time to cook the cake: 30 minutes

Time for the cake to cool down: 1 hour

Total time spent for the process: 1 hour and 45 minutes

Equipment That Will Be Needed

  • Oven
  • Mixing Bowls
  • Cake Pan
  • Any Mixer(Hand or Electric)
  • Measuring Cups/Spoons
  • Optional: Oven Mittens

Ingredients To Be Used For The Cake Mix

Ingredient Amount
Butter/Baking Spray Varies
All-Purpose Flour 2 Cups
Baking Powder 3/4 Teaspoon
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Large Eggs 5 count
Granulated Sugar 1 and 1/4 Cups
Whole Milk 1/2 Cup
Vanilla Extract 2 Teaspoons

Ingredients To Be Used For The Syrup Mix

Ingredient Amount
Sweetened Condensed Milk 1 can
Evaporated Milk 1 can
Whole Milk 2/3 Cup

Ingredients To Be Used For The Frosting

Ingredient Amount
Heavy Whipping Cream 2 cups
Powdered Sugar 2 tablespoons
Fresh Strawberries Varies by choice

Directions To Make The Cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F. Take your cake pan and butter it or spray with baking spray.
  2. Take a large bowl and whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. The eggs will be separated into two different bowls. 1 bowl will be a medium bowl to hold the yolks and another bowl will be a large bowl to hold the whites.
  4. Beat the whites in the large bowl until they are foamy which usually takes 1 minute. As you are doing this you will slowly pour in 1/4 cups of sugar and continue beating for roughly another 3 minutes.
  5. In the bowl containing the egg yolks, add in the remaining 1 cup of sugar and beat it all together until it is pale and fluffy which is roughly 1 minute.
  6. Still on the medium bowl, add in the whole milk and vanilla extract and whisk it all together, once that is done combine it together with the large bowl that has the flour, baking powder, and salt and mix until just combined. WARNING: DO NOT OVERMIX!
  7. With this new flour mixture we will now combine it together with the remaining large bowl that holds the egg whites. You will then fold it all together until a soft fluffy batter remains ready to go into the cake pan.
  8. Take the batter and pour into a buttered or sprayed cake pan making sure it is as layered as possible. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes and upon finishing let the cake cool in a slightly warm place.
Disclosure

As the cake is cooling down it will seem to be shrinking or falling, but it is important to remember that this is supposed to happen and it is normal no need for any action.

Directions To Make The Syrup

  1. Take a medium bowl and whisk together the condensed milk, evaporated milk, and the whole milk until it is well combined.
  2. Take a fork and poke all the top of the cake and once the top is all poked slowly pour the syrup all over the top then cover and let the cake cool for at least 1 hour.

Directions To Make The Frosting

  1. Take a large bowl and beat together the heavy whipping cream and powdered sugar until soft peaks form.
  2. Take that mixture and spread all over the cake. Now put the cake into the refrigerator, you may cover the cake if you wish so and add strawberries upon your liking.
Disclosure

Please note you must leave the cake refrigerated for at least 3 days before you serve it. The tres leches cake tastes best when cold, this time frame allows the best chance for the cake to soak up the syrup for the best result possible.

Disclosure

Not every attempt at making this tres leches cake will result in perfected results and each cake may appear different than the last. This recipe is the framework needed to create it and each person may adjust the recipe to fit their tastes.

Congragulations you have successfully made a delicious tres leches cake, remember this recipe serves as your template and you may adjust it to your liking or to the liking of others as you see fit.
